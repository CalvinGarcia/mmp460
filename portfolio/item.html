Foreigner : A Travel Magazine

Foreigner is a quarterly magazine which shows its readers the best places to go while abroad in order to capture a sense of living like a local. This Magazine has two parts. The cover, and a two page interior spread centered on Osaka Japan, and its various nooks and crannies.
